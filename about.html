About Us

Kinetix was founded by an industry leader with a team established in 1986 that has worked together for up to 20 years.


Our vendor and client relationships cover every country in the world and have existed for up to 30 years. Clients include corporations, U.S. and foreign governments, and global non-governmental organizations.

These relationships reflect the trust our clients have in Kinetix and our integrity, consistency, and expertise. Our incredibly high employee retention proves that our core values guide our operations every day. Our employees grow with our clients, and develop a keen understanding of their strategy and objectives. We expect no less from our partners around the world, and have positioned Kinetix to leverage our volume and coverage to our client’s benefit, every time.

Our team has an average tenure in their specialty of over 20 years, operating in service, government and corporate environments. We provide expert support for the strategic growth and development of our clients through the provision of services in over 160 countries worldwide.

Our valued clients choose Kinetix International because we maintain a totally flexible platform when providing best in class service in all that we deliver. Each of our client relationships is custom built to support the business and economic strategies of the client in each of our core competencies, regardless of which industry we support. All of our client partnerships are founded on the application of key metrics with commitments in the areas of service delivery, cost transparency and complete accountability.

Whether your requirements include relocating an employee across town or around the world, handling project cargo logistics and freight forwarding for government agencies, or some of the largest government funded logistics contracts in existence, buyers in search of combined service enhancements with defined cost controls choose Kinetix International.

"Kinetix is the choice for cost effectiveness and seamless service delivery."

Shayne Berlo

Executive Vice President

Shayne’s primary responsibility is to maximize the strategic focus, operating performance and financial goals of Kinetix.

He has spent 20 years within the USAID, DOS, and DOD logistics industry as both a Project Manager on several multi-million USAID contracts and as a Business Development Director. Shayne will act as liaison between clients and Kinetix staff to insure contract compliance and customer service is maintained at maximum levels. Shayne will delegate specific authority, responsibility, and accountability across the organization by establishing clear lines of responsibility for each functional area of a scope of work.

Shayne is an active member of the Society for International Development, a specialist with FAR, DFAR, U.S. Fly American Act and MARAD Cargo Preference regulations and can assist clients with compliance.

Director of Relocation Services
