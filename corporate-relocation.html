Corporate Relocation

“World-Class Relocation Services for Global Companies”

Kinetix Relocation specializes in the design and delivery of innovative programs for corporations of all sizes. Whether our valued clients transfer the occasional new hire or have a mature program that relocates senior executives on a regular basis, Kinetix is prepared to deliver a best in class process to ensure a smooth and cost effective move anywhere in the world.


Kinetix Relocation understands the stress families experience during this trying time and have designed a program which alleviates much of the stress factors. By committing a single point of accountability to employee and corporation alike, Kinetix acts as advocate in the detailed management of each service offered to our client’s mobile workforce.


Kinetix Relocation is committed to building a custom program for our valued clients in order that both their business and financial strategies are met. Utilizing a worldwide network of independent service partners, Kinetix is able to design, deliver and report on our services around the corner or around the world!

Consultation & Financial Services

Policy Review, Design & Construction

Policy Review, Design & Construction

Kinetix Relocation understands that they key foundation for both a service and cost effective relocation program is a clear and precise corporate policy. In order that Kinetix Relocation can deliver Best in Class service, it is imperative that we understand a corporation’s culture, their goals and objectives from both an employee and strategic business perspective, and their requirements for complete accountability. By operating within this formula for execution, Kinetix is prepared to adhere to the guidelines of a finely tuned policy, or assist our valued clients in the design and development of a unique and custom built management tool. We understand the differences between vertical markets and are able to apply accurate and effective processes in order that our clients deliver the most appropriate entitlement package to their mobile workforce. Our expert staff has the ability to benchmark both vertical markets and the industry in general, prior to prescribing a new or revised corporate policy.

Cost of Living Analysis (COLA)

Cost of Living Analysis (COLA)

The basic premise of a corporate relocation policy is to ensure that the transferred employee is kept financially whole during and after their relocation process has been completed. In order to accomplish this, Kinetix Relocation is prepared to manage a Cost of Living Analysis process that will meet the transferee’s needs, while maintaining the fiduciary responsibility to our clients.

Tax & Expense Management

Tax & Expense Management

The complexities of corporate relocation can disrupt the normal functioning of an employer’s accounting, human resources, accounts payable and payroll departments. Through our valued partnership with American Escrow & Closing Company, Kinetix Relocation is able to ensure that transferee’s relocation expenses are managed efficiently and our client’s maintain IRS compliance and receive complete financial accountability.


Domestic Services Portfolio

Home Sale Programs

Home Sale Programs

Kinetix Relocation understands that the sale of a transferring family’s home is typically the largest line item cost, but we don’t lose sight of the emotional separation often felt by the family unit. We are sensitive to all aspects of the home sale process, and through our network of pre-qualified home sale specialists, we deliver a streamlined program which results in comfort for the family and cost efficiency for the client. Kinetix is prepared to deliver custom programs including Direct Reimbursement, Buyer Value Options (BVO) and Amended Value programs (AV). Maintaining IRS compliance is critical in this process.

Household Goods Move Management

Household Goods Move Management

Kinetix Relocation maintains an independent working relationship with a select roster of pre-qualified moving companies. Kinetix looks beyond national contracts and manages local and regional movers in order to ensure successful pack, load, and storage and delivery services. Through Odyssey, a proprietary database, we are able to control costs, track timelines, and effect risk management. Our transportation specialists adhere to our clients’ corporate policies, thus ensuring a smooth and cost efficient program for transferee and corporate alike.

Property Management Assistance

Property Management Assistance

American expatriates often decide to keep their homes during overseas assignments, and unstable real estate markets can cause a domestic transferee to question whether the sale of their home is prudent at the time of their move. Kinetix Relocation is prepared to manage both vacant homes and rental properties for both our domestic and international clients. Through our Property Management and Rental Management Programs, Kinetix performs the fiduciary task of maintaining the financial and structural integrity of the expatriate’s home or the loss remediation of a domestic home during downturns in the real estate market.

Orientation and Settling-In Support

Orientation and Settling-In Support

Kinetix Relocation understands that returning to a state of normalcy is vital toward a smooth and efficient transition into the new home and community. Kinetix is prepared to manage this process via our network of ‘on the ground’ consultants in most destination locations. Whether it is discovering the community infrastructure, introductions to social and cultural sources, banking, driver and automobile matters, etc., our goal is to assist our transferring families in a full return to a productive work life and consistent lifestyle.

Temporary Living

Temporary Living

Transferees and their families deserve a soft entry into the new home community while they become familiar with the new work place and assimilate into the new living community. Kinetix Relocation maintains a network of independent temporary housing providers that deliver quality interim properties with great amenities as the family’s ‘first stop’ en route to their new home. With the Kinetix temporary housing program, our transferring families are ‘never far from home”!

Home Purchase Assistance

Home Purchase Assistance

At Kinetix Relocation, the Relocation Consultant that supports the transferee in the sale of their home will also oversee the home finding process. Understanding and maintaining the complete timeline of a transferee’s relocation results in consistency to family requirements and adherence to corporate direction. Kinetix maintains an independent roster of home finding specialists that act as buyer advocates for the transferee with the understanding that an effective purchase will result in a smoother subsequent home sale experience for the career employee.

Rental Assistance

Rental Assistance

Kinetix Relocation understands that not every transferee is a home owner, or market condition often dictates that a home owner rent versus buy if the real estate climate dictates. Kinetix maintains a roster of independent agents that specialize in rentals, as the usual and customary practice of rentals varies from state to state. Our end agents understand urban versus suburban, rental community versus hi-rise apartment buildings, etc. Our Relocation Consultants are versed in property search, lease negotiation and execution and amenities/utilities consultation.

Mortgage Assistance

Mortgage Assistance

Kinetix Relocation partners with several national mortgage lenders that maintain divisions well versed in relocation mortgage products. From pre-qualification to short time approval, competitive mortgage packages to direct client billing, Kinetix understands the transferee’s needs for a fast and efficient corporate mortgage program!


International Services Portfolio

Departure Counseling

Departure Counseling

Kinetix Relocation understands that the foundation for a successful overseas assignment is the transferees’ complete understanding of the program and what are defined expectations. Our menu of departure services are custom built for each transferee within the guidelines set forth by the corporation. Included are pre-assignment and budget counseling, policy consultation, visa & immigration support, tax & expense management, etc.

Visa & Immigration Support

Visa & Immigration Support

Every international work assignment commences with an understanding of the visa & immigration requirements necessary for a transferee to work stateside or anywhere in the world. It is vital that Kinetix partner with proven experts in this sensitive phase of the transfer process. Our service partners maintain a deep understanding of country specific labor laws and all requirements needing completion which can impact the transferee’s (and family’s) living and working in a specific country and the employer’s compliance with business and employment law.

Cross Cultural Training, Language Training and Spouse/Partner & Children’s Support

Cross Cultural Training, Language Training and Spouse/Partner & Children’s Support

Corporations work diligently to provide vital support to the transferee and family alike when preparing for an overseas assignment. It is crucial that all understand how to assimilate into the host country fabric via a cultural understanding, which of course includes a rapid ability to communicate with native citizens. Similarly, it is important that the cultural, social, schooling and employment assimilation is handled in the same manner. Kinetix works with on the ground experts in both the home and host countries in order to return all visitors to a state of normalcy and productivity as quickly as possible.

International Household Goods Move Management

International Household Goods Move Management

Temporary Living

Temporary Living

Arriving into temporary accommodations in one’s home country is difficult enough, but when you factor in a new country the task can be more daunting and stressful. Kinetix understands how varied international temporary living can be, and maintains an in-house team well versed in identifying and securing appropriate living quarters for transferee’s on overseas assignment in order that they can remain productive upon arrival in the host country, while living comfortably while they search for their permanent residences.

Destination Services

Destination Services

Kinetix understands that navigating host country specific regulations, social/cultural and language differences, new surroundings, etc. can be both difficult and frustrating. In order to help transferees on overseas assignments, Kinetix has partnered with on the ground experts throughout the world to provide vital support in services including area orientation, home finding assistance, settling-in and a menu of personal support programs.

Auto Lease / Purchase Programs

Auto Lease / Purchase Programs

Leasing or purchasing an automobile for a foreign national entering the US without credit history has been a constant dilemma. Kinetix partners with specialists who can design both structured lease and purchase programs, or open ended packages which offer flexibility in costs, terms and auto manufacturers.

International Banking Services

International Banking Services

Opening bank accounts and securing credit without history has been a difficult task for foreign nationals entering the US. Kinetix has partnered with banking experts who can work with incoming transferees in advance of their arrival to ensure a smooth financial transition in order to be able to conduct their banking business upon arrival to the US. Arriving expatriates will have access to credit, including traditional charge cards, home mortgages, etc.
